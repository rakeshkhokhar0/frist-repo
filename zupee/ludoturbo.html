Speed Ludo: Rules on Ludo Turbo
Ludo Turbo rules are slightly different from the classic Ludo game rules:
  • All the tokens are in the open position. You don't need a 6 to begin the game.
  • All the players get the same number of values but in a different order.
  • All players get an equal number of moves to play and have 10 seconds to make the move.
  • The game has a limited number of moves, so make sure you plan each of your moves carefully. It may break or make the game for you!
  • You lose a turn every time you fail to make a move within the stipulated 10 seconds.
  • If you skip three times, you will be out of the game.
  • You also get an extra turn when you cut an opponent's token and also every time one of your tokens reaches home.
  • If the token is cut, all points made on that token are lost.
