Ludo Online - Ludo Ninja Rules
Ludo Supreme gold rules are slightly different from the classic Ludo game rules:

  • All Ninja players get an equal number of moves and have 10 seconds to make each move.
  • Every player gets an equal number of 1s, 2s, 3s, 4s, 5s, and 6s, just in a different order.
  • You lose a turn every time you fail to make a move within the stipulated 10 seconds. So, if you skip three times, you will be out of the game.

  • The player with the highest score wins the game. In case of a tie, the payout is split.
